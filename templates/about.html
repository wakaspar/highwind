About Me

My name is Will Kaspar and I'm a web developer based out of San Francisco, California. I've been coding since 2016, working in both JavaScript and Ruby. In that time, I've managed to learn a handful of other useful fullstack technologies, such as NodeJs and Express, Rails, AngularJs, SaSS, and more.

I'm very passionate about developing software, as it gives me a great outlet for my creativity while allowing me to chase my interests in technology. Web development is the perfect balance of design and technology to pique my interest. Hopefully, as you explore this website, that passion comes through :)

When I'm not building software, I'm passionate about the outdoors. I grew up in southeastern Tennessee and absolutely adore the mountains. I thoroughly enjoy hiking, camping, and backpacking. I have a dog, Indigo, who's a 5-year-old cattle beagle mix and my most loyal companion. Before tech, I worked as a residential gardener here in SF, and I still enjoy gardening - it's become one of my favorite hobbies. I also love pinball and all kinds of gaming.

My favorite food is pepperoni pizza.
